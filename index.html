About the Firm

This demo site demonstrates how a Chartered Accountant firm may present particulars on its website while following the guidelines issued by the ICAI. The website operates on a pull model: details such as specific areas of experience, nature of services, number of articled assistants and assignment details are shown only after a deliberate click by the visitor.

No banner advertisements or promotional solicitations are displayed on this website.
